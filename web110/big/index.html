What did I Learn from this Project.

This project taught me a lot about putting together a quality responsive website and I think I learned more about myself.

First, the website. It was cool to put together a site that I am proud of. I like that we covered so many different aspects of what can be in a site. From the basics of index page to decent navigation. Learning how to put a map , calender and photo gallery in. While doing a little research on what will make me a better and more well rounded Web Developer. The thing I liked most about building the site is that we never forgot that we will be doing this for a client.

Second, me, that is why I put the inspirational quotes in the <aside>. It has been a while since I have been in school and the biggest thing I learned is that with significant persistence even a daunting task can be accomplish with a measure of confidence and competence. It has been a fun experience and it all makes me really look forward to the schooling I have left.
